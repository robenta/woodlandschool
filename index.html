The Proprietress

At Woodland Schools, we pride ourselves in character building and achieving excellence with total care. That is why we are open minded, accommodating and not anti-change. We aim at making the children aware of their own potential and to instill in the children the importance of self-pride and confidence, so that they can stand as equals with their counter parts from any part of the world. We also know that children are the future and the promise of the future is an awesome promise, so we try to bring out the best in each child both morally and academically by giving them encouragement and teaching them well so that they can lead the way in our ever changing world. For us at Woodland, learning is not about what you get, it is about what you become. This website provides information to questions you may have concerning our school policies and procedures. If you still have questions about anything, please do not hesitate to contact the school office. We look forward to partnering with you as we endeavour to bring out the best in each child.

Why choose Woodland?

Because you want the best for your child. Of all the benefits you can give your son or daughter, none has a more lasting value than a good education at school where he or she will be happy and successful. Woodland is a school where the staff care and each child is treated as a unique individual. Our vision is to prepare pupils and students for a life of leadership, service and personal fulfillment. In a bid to achieve our mission, we ensure that our staff are qualified individuals who not only love and understand children but are also firm believers in our school's motto, “Hard work is all”.

Woodland Montessori

Our Montessori school offers a multi-age-level approach to learning. Students remain with a single teacher for the duration. This allows strong bonds to form between the teacher and child, between the teacher and the child's parents, and between students.
